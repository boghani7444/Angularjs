What is Hosting Assured Program?

  • Hosting Assured seal is TheWebHostingDir.com's seal of customer excellence. To display the Hosting Assured seal on your site you must agree to abide by a code of customer ethics.
  • Hosting Assured seal is an ethics trust mark that hundreds of web hosting companies display on their website and agree to abide by.
  • Hosting Assured is not a certification or award.
  • Hosting Assured gathers praise comments from your customers which are then available to anybody who clicks on the seal on your site. Hosting Assured also offers to mediate complaints should an unsolveable problem arise.

What if a problem arises between customer and web hosting company?

 If a problem arises between a customer and Hosting Assured Host, and if customer cannot resolve it directly with the web hosting company.
  • Hosting Assured can help.
  • Hosting Assured cannot guarantee a solution will be reached, but we will do our best to help mediate the problem.
  • If you (web host) does not abide by the code of ethics in their dealings with customer, then your Hosting Assured status will be removed.
  • This provides you with an additional level of peace of mind to customer.

How The Hosting Assured Trust Mark Functions On Your Site?

When a visitor to your website clicks on your valid Hosting Assured trust mark logo they do not get transported to this site. A special Hosting Assured site that relates to your company is opened. This shows your Hosting Assured status, your current customer rating score plus access to the detailed ratings and the ability to check your reliability.
